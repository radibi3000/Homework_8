Resume

As a technology and cognitive psychology enthusiast, I have always been interested in understanding users' ideas and visions to improve online platforms and products. I have worked in several organizations, including start-up companies and non-profit organizations, where I always strive to engage thoughtfully in every aspect of the UX research process. I am drawn to user research because it connects my passions at the intersection of psychology and technology. Through the Design Thinking process, I aspire to create socially responsible and equitable products that enhance social justice initiatives and improve user accessibility. Through UX research, I aspire to understand and bolster people's unique histories, social contexts, and opinions. Outside of UX research, I enjoy studying philosophy, hiking, listening to music, and watching movies. I am a first-generation Iranian American and my culture and heritage are a huge part of my identity and interests. Please feel free to reach out; I look forward to connecting!
