What was the goal:

For my design studio course at Carnegie Mellon we had an assignment to create a website for pet adoption that fit actual users needs. The goal of this website was to create a platform that had a responsive display, specifically over iOS, web, and apple watch interface.

What methods did we use:

We conducted:

1. Qualitative interviews with pet owners to learn about their customer journey
2. Directed Storytelling to gain more insights on their pain points
3. Think Aloud Studies to test out our low-fidelity wireframes

What did I learn:

I learned how to conduct interviews in an academic fashion while following deliverable guidelines, as well as working cross-functionally with designers to promote my research findings and ultimately implement them.
